## 🎯 Project Overview
This project transforms raw sales data into a prescriptive business model. It covers the full analytical lifecycle from data cleaning to risk simulation.

## 🛠️ Key Analytical Steps
1. **The Brain Sheet:** Built a dynamic data architecture linking unit costs, prices, and margins to the master dataset.
2. **Descriptive Statistics:** Analyzed central tendency (Mean/Median) and dispersion to understand data distribution.
3. **Hypothesis Testing:** Conducted a Two-Sample t-Test (P-value: 0.27) to prove that sales rep performance differences were statistically insignificant.
4. **Linear Regression:** Tested the relationship between Discounts and Sales. Discovered an R-Square of 0.0005, proving discounts do not drive revenue in this model.
5. **Optimization (Solver):** Used Simplex LP to find the "Global Optimum" for a $50,000 budget, identifying a maximum profit of $49,980.
6. **Risk Simulation:** Used Scenario Manager to stress-test the plan, revealing a 94% profit risk during high-inflation scenarios.

## 💡 Final Recommendation
While the mathematical "Optimal" suggests 100% investment in Food, the **Sensitivity Analysis** suggests a diversified strategy to protect against cost volatility.
